We love code and soldering !

We believe in today’s world the best way to learn is by working together helping each other grow. Our workshops provides an environment to learn by collaborating in a team, making future engineers ready for big industrial projects and having fun while learning and making projects that matters.





1. Drone Training program : Helping build future drone pilots for different domains ranging from Aerial cinematography, sports-racing, Content creation, surveying-mapping, first person flying using our in house elite simulators for RC fixed wing and multi rotors drones.

2. Faculty Development Program(FDP) : A special one day program for faculty members to introduce them with latest education technologies(EDTECH) that they can use in their classes to make it more interesting and interactive

3. Career counselling : Exclusively for prospective and future engineering students helping them decide their career paths over 1-1 counselling .
